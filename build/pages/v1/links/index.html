THE LOGOSOME (Resources and Initial Attempts)

Sites providing etymological, lexical, and semantic resources that could be linked to texts or other expressive artifacts of known provenance (time, place, authorship etc) to facilitate the study of the interplay between meanings and their vehicles of expression over time and across cultures. How mankind has parcelled reality up, and then tried to tie it back together.
