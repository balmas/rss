Alpheios builds evidence-based, open-source software to support worldwide study of classical languages and literatures.

Alpheios Reading Tools

The Alpheios Reading Tools provide reading aids for any Latin or Ancient Greek text on the web, with experimental support for Classical Arabic and Persian. Our alpha release of Alpheios Version 2 is now available.

Install

To try the tools with any text, please use a supported browser.

  • Download Chrome
  • Download Firefox

Please access this page in a supported browser on the desktop to install Alpheios. Mobile solutions are coming soon.

Our Goals

Our immediate goal is to make all of the functionality from the original Alpheios Version 1 Firefox extensions available in Version 2 for major browsers and on mobile devices, expanding at the same time to support more languages, including Persian, Syriac and Hebrew. Longer term our goals are manifold, from enhancing the reading experience to include audio and imagery as well as linguistic aids and games, to developing a deep, data-backed understanding of how and why people learn languages and what the effect is on the brain.
